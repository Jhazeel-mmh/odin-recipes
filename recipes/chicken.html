Breaded Chicken with Mashed Potatoes

This recipe is for two persons and it takes around 53 minutes to be done.

Ingredients

  • 4 Potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of cream (Ideal Cremosita)
  • 1/2 Cup of Water
  • 4 Slices of Shicken
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Breadcrumbs
  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of Nutmeg
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 1 pinch of table salt

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes in water until they are soft. Peel them and mash them.
  2. Put the mashed potatoes in a pot with 1/2 cup of cream, water, and stir constantly. Add the butter and adjust the seasoning.
  3. Dip the chicken fillets in beaten egg and then in the Breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan. Brown the breaded chicken over medium heat. Serve with the mashed potatoes.
